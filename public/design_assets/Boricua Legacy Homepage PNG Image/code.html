Meet the Author

A Lifelong Dedication to Storytelling

Born in the mountains of Utuado and raised in the diaspora, Jacqueline has spent over two decades researching the intersection of gender, culture, and resistance in the Caribbean.

"Boricua Legacy" is the culmination of years of archival digging and oral interviews, bringing to light the women who were written out of the textbooks but remained the backbone of the island.
